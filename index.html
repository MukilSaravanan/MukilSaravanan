About Me

Mukil Saravanan is a graduate researcher at the Robotics Innovations Lab in Indian Institute of Science, Bangalore. His research interest lies robot navigation, computer vision, with a special emphasis on bringing intelligence to robots. He is a recipient of multiple awards and a fellowship including the leaderboard prizes in flagship IEEE ICRA, IROS 2022 competitions, the prestigious Summer Research Fellowship by Indian Academy of Sciences (2021), and the top 4 finalists in Synopsys Inno Champ (2020).

He earned his bachelor's degree in Electronics and Communication Engineering from the Government College of Technology, Coimbatore where he chaired the student branch to be honoured for numerous outreach activities through competitions and seminars.

Apart from his academic endeavours, he enjoys photographing landscapes, streets and loves beatboxing and callisthenics.
